We, Bonsai Nepal, situated at Kathmandu, Nepal, is a young venture with the moto of bringing Bonsai plants to every house in the city. We have been successful in providing the quality service since 2022. With the blessings of the Mother Earth we have created bonsai trees that are today part of many homes.The plants designed here of international bonsai standard but with local touch.The Founder Praneet Tuladhar says “every breath we breathe is because of the trees and dedicating the entire life to these trees is a blessing. Just like how our parents give us life, the Tree gives us life giving oxygen to breathe, so taking care of them is an individual’s duty.”

Bonsai ("planting in a pot" meaning in Japanese) are trees in a bonsai pot designed using certain techniques. Bonsai consists of the two word parts Bon=pot and Sai=plant. Bonsai Nepal one stop go for all types of bonsai tree species. All bonsai are individually photographed and after a purchase in our online shop, you get exactly the tree you have ordered. Safely packed, the tree will be delivered quickly as possible.
